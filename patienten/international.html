How it works

From your country to our practice –
structured at every step.

You don't need to travel to get a first opinion. We start remotely and only ask you to travel when it makes clinical sense.

  • 01

    Remote pre-assessment

    Send us your imaging (MRI, X-rays), reports and previous treatment history by email or secure file transfer. We review and respond within 48 hours with an initial clinical assessment.

  • 02

    Video second opinion

    A structured video consultation with Dr. Mosafer – in English, Arabic or Farsi. We review your case in detail, discuss options and give you a written summary. No travel required for this step.

  • 03

    In-person visit (if indicated)

    If surgery or in-person examination is needed, we coordinate your appointment, assist with logistics planning and ensure everything is prepared for your arrival.

  • 04

    Surgery & hospital stay

    Surgery at Klinikum Traunstein (from July 2026). Typical hospital stay 5–10 days depending on procedure. We coordinate your care team, interpreter support and discharge planning.

  • 05

    Video follow-up from home

    After you return home, we continue structured follow-up via video. You send imaging digitally; we review and provide written reports. No need to travel for routine check-ups.

Revision surgery

Logistics

Practical information for your stay.

Location

Mosafer Spine, Rosenheim – 60 km southeast of Munich, easily reachable from Munich Airport (MUC) by train or car.

Language

Dr. Mosafer speaks English, Arabic and Farsi fluently. Written reports can be issued in English.

Costs & insurance

International patients are treated on a private/self-pay basis. We issue detailed cost estimates in advance. We work with international insurance companies and can provide necessary documentation for reimbursement claims.

Visa support

We can provide invitation letters and medical documentation to support your visa application for Germany.

Send your records

Email imaging and reports to our practice. We accept DICOM files, CDs and digital transfers. We will confirm receipt and provide an assessment timeline within 24 hours.

Response time

Remote pre-assessment: within 48 hours. Video consultation: within 1 week. In-person appointment: within 2–4 weeks depending on urgency.

Emergency cases

For urgent neurological symptoms (cauda equina syndrome, progressive paralysis), please state this clearly in your message. We prioritise urgent cases.
