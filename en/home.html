What we treat

Specialised care, not general orthopaedics.

Three clinical pillars, one continuous relationship with Dr. Mosafer – not with rotating teams.

01 · Scoliosis

Scoliosis & spinal deformity

From first diagnosis and bracing through surgical correction – for adolescents, adults and children with growth-guiding systems (MAGEC, NEMOST, VBT).

02 · Spine Surgery

Spinal surgery

Disc herniation, stenosis, minimally invasive fusion (TLIF/XLIF/OLIF), motion-preservation (PEEK rod) and specialised revision surgery.

03 · Conservative

Conservative & biological therapy

Image-guided injections (PRT, facet joint), biological procedures (PRP/ACP) and shockwave therapy – applied when clearly indicated.

Principles

How we work.

01

Conservative first

Surgery only when structured alternatives have been exhausted.

02

Honest counselling

Clear presentation of alternatives, limits and realistic outcomes.

03

Personal continuity

Continuous care by Dr. Mosafer, not by rotating teams.

04

Long follow-up

Systematic follow-up over at least 12 months; for children over many years.
